Diagnostic Test Evaluation

(website currently under initial development)

This website accompanies a report entitled "Defining and Using Reference Standards for New Diagnostic Tests for Neglected Tropical Diseases", which is the result of a 2023-2024 project designed to provide an overview of concepts and recommended methods for estimating the performance of new diagnostic methods for neglected tropical diseases (NTDs), as well as guidance for how to approach the evaluation of diagnostic tests under the wide range of applications relevant to NTDs. The approaches outlined are influenced by requirements identified by the target product profiles (TPP) produced by the diagnostic and technical advisory group (DTAG) of the World Health Organization (WHO), as well as previous work identified from the wider scientific literature.
